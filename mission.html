Cutting-Edge Creamery

Our Mission

Cutting-Edge Creamery strives to build strong connections within the community through the love of ice cream. We have developed a twist (more of a roll) to the classic summer treat that will be sure to surprise you. By creating a food truck, we can now become a part of your everyday lives in your everyday locations. We enjoy being apart of your community and we want to provide something special that will make your memories last. Whether you're taking a break between classes or celebrating something special, we love being apart of your day.
